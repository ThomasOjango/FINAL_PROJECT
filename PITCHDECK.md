# Umoja Hub - Pitch Deck

## Problem Statement

Kenya faces a critical food security crisis:
- **2.3 million** Kenyans are food insecure
- **26%** of children suffer from stunting due to malnutrition
- Millions of smallholder farmers lose surplus crops annually
- Communities lack efficient ways to connect surplus food with those in need
- Lack of accessible platforms for coordinated food donation efforts

## The Gap

Current solutions are fragmented:
- Food banks operate in silos without community connection
- Farmers have no easy way to share surplus
- Donors struggle to make impact-driven contributions
- Limited education on affordable nutrition and food waste reduction

## Our Solution: Umoja Hub

**Umoja Hub** is a unified digital platform that brings together farmers, donors, and communities to fight hunger in Kenya.

### Core Features

**1. Food Sharing Network**
- Farmers and shops list surplus food instantly
- Communities browse available food with location and contact details
- Reduces food waste while reaching those in need
- Zero transaction fees

**2. Unified Donation Platform**
- Accept food donations with convenient drop-off centers
- Accept monetary donations via M-Pesa, Airtel Money, PayPal
- Track impact of contributions in real-time
- Simple, user-friendly interface

**3. Education & Empowerment**
- Affordable healthy eating tips
- Food waste reduction strategies
- Support for local farmers
- Community resources and agricultural guidance

**4. Strategic Reach**
- Mobile-first design for accessibility across Kenya
- Collection centers in major cities (Nairobi, Mombasa, Kisumu, Nakuru)
- Direct integration with local communities
